Decision

This pieces is an exploration of decisions resulting in two of the strongest emotions which will experience in our lifetime, hope and despair. Just as love conquers hate, hope can conquer despair. Even when Pandora opened the box and let all of the evils and chaos into the world, in the end, there was still hope. It is ever present; always there; the light at the end of the tunnel. In the deepest depths of despair, there is the glimmer of hope. Reach out those hands and you will find hope, rising above e ready to lift you up and reassure you that everything will be okay, and remind you that even the darkest nights are still greeted by the brightest dawns.
